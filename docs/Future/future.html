Conclusion


IoT technology is only getting increasingly ubiquitous in everyday life and does not seem to be going away anytime soon. While there are benefits to be gained from the technology, the security and ethical concerns cannot be ignored. It is a fundamentally flawed system that has the potential to improve the daily life of every human on earth, but because of that expansive ability, makes it practically impossible to lock down securely, or regulate to ensure privacy. Research in the future can investigate new architectures and systems that may be able to alleviate one or both major risks, but as of right now, an IoT future is potentially one of overreaching corporate control.
